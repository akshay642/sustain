About Event:

Are you ready to disrupt the status quo? Hosted by the Department of BBA and BBA in Business Analytics, Hackathon 2090 is the stage where you can flex your intellectual muscles. Join us at PESU 52 on October 13 and 14, 2023, and be part of the momentum that's driving real change. This is where your ideas can literally shape the world.

2090 Innovate-2-Sustain

Hackathon Theme:

Tired of just tweeting about the UN's 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)? Here's your chance to actually do something about them. Collaborate, innovate, and create solutions that will make the planet a better place. Your ideas have power; unleash them.

2090 Innovate-2-Sustain

Objectives:

The 'Hackathon' encourages individuals to level up their game in the sustainability arena, innovate from within, receive coaching from industry leaders, turn dream projects into actionable plans, and network with driven individuals to potentially find co-founders.

Registration & Team Formation: A Unique Approach for Maximized Impact

Two's Company

Register in pairs, because two minds are better than one. You and your partner will bring your specialized skills to the table, setting the stage for groundbreaking solutions.

Interdisciplinary Teams

Your duo will be strategically combined with two other pairs, forming a supergroup of six. This isn't just a team; it's a powerhouse of diverse expertise, tailored to tackle the most pressing issues.

Curated Challenges

We've got real-world problems from leading organizations. Each team's unique skill mix will be aligned with a problem statement that demands your collective talents. You're not just filling a spot; you're completing a puzzle.

Broaden Your Horizons

This is your chance to collaborate outside your comfort zone. Learn from peers in other disciplines, expanding your perspective and skillset.

Level Up Your Network

The team formation process isn't random; it's calculated for success. Your teammates could be future business partners, co-founders, or lifelong friends.

Why Settle for One Mentor?

With an interdisciplinary team, you're not just getting one mentor's advice; you're tapping into a whole network of industry leaders relevant to your team's diverse fields.

Maximized Impact

Your team won't just be diverse; it will be dynamic. Imagine a blend of tech-savvy, business acumen, and creative ingenuity all aimed at creating sustainable solutions. That's your team.

Be the Missing Link

You could be the key expertise your team needs to create the next big sustainable solution. Don't miss out on this opportunity to make a real-world impact.
